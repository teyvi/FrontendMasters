How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich

  1. Get bread
  2. Make two 4 inch bread slices
  3. Fetch peanut butter and Jelly
  4. Use cutlery knife to scoop peanut butter and spread on one bread
  5. Wipe knife on a paper towel and scoop jelly and spread on the other bread
  6. Pick both bread with their spreads facing each other together
  7. There you go! Your very own peanut butter and jelly sandwich
